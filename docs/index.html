I am a physicist studying gravity. My current position is research fellow at the School of Mathematical Sciences of the University of Nottingham. Previously, I was a PhD student at Instituto Superior Tecnico in Lisbon, and a member of the CENTRA/Grit research group.

My main research focus is on methods that are appropriate for numerical simulations of gravitational systems. So far, I have been exploring the mathematical properties of hyperbolicity and well-posedness of characteristic formulations of general relativity, and their numerical applications. In brief, these formulations are based on light-rays and have been used for instance in the modeling of gravitational waves, as well as of strongly interacting plasmas via holography.
